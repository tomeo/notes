# Quesa Birria Tacos

## Ingredients

| Ingredient              | Amount   | Notes |
|-------------------------|----------|-------|
| Chuck Roast             | 1.5 kg   | Fancier option: Short rib or a third each of chuck, short rib and oxtail |
| Dried Ancho Chili       | 30 g     | 5 chilis ≈ 30 g |
| Dried Guajillo Chili    | 30 g     | 5 chilis ≈ 30 g |
| Chipotle in Adobo sauce | 1 pc     | Also use the adobo sauce instead of tomato paste! |
| Beef Stock              | 2.25 L   | The pros say make your own! |
| Tomato Paste            | 12 g     | 12 g ≈ 1 tbsp, can be replaced with adobo sauce |
| Yellow Onion            | 2 pc     | 1 for cooking, 1 for serving |
| Garlic                  | 8 cloves | |
| Bay Leaves              | 6 pc     | |
| Cinnamon Stick          | 1 pc     | |
| Black Pepper            | 8 g      | 8 g ≈ 1 tbsp |
| Toasted Coriander       | 5 g      | 5 g ≈ 1 tbsp, ground coriander works OK |
| Fresh Coriander         | 1 bch    | For serving |
| Shredded Mozarella      | 500g     | Or mexican cheese if you can get it |

## Steps

1. Prepare dried chilis + Prepare meat
2. Prepare broth
3. Blend broth
4. Let simmer for an additional 90 minutes or until your meat is very tender
5. Discard bones, shred meat
6. Season leftover broth with salt
7. Make tacos
8. Make cups of broth, add fresh koriander and onion to the broth cup
9. Eat

### Prepare dried chilis

1. Take dried chilis, remove stems, deseed, rip into flat sections
2. Toast the dried chilis in a frying pan. No oil. Let them change color
3. Boil water. Once boiling turn off heat and place the chilis in the water for 20 minutes to rehydrate.
4. If you are making stock from cubes, use the water from rehydrating the chilis.

### Prepare meat

1. Don't remove any marbled fat
2. Cut into chunks of about 5x5 cm
3. Season meat with salt and let sit for 20 minutes
4. Sear meat in a big pot for for 2-3 minutes on each side until you have a nice deep brown crust
5. Dont wash the pot, instead put the meat to the side and start preparing your broth in the same pot

### Prepare broth

1. Add 1 chopped onion to the pot and sauté until it begins to soften
2. Add 1 tbsp of tomato paste (or use adobo sauce)
3. Let cook down until paste starts to darken
4. Add 8 cloves of sliced garlic (could be lightly toasted before)
5. Sauté until fragrant
6. Add 2.25 L of beef stock
7. Add the rehydrated dried chilis
8. Add 8 g of black pepper, 5 g of toasted coriander, 1 cinnamon stick and 6 bay leaves
9. Add the meat again
10. Let braise for 1 hour, stirring occasionally

### Blend broth

1. Remove dried chilis from broth and place in blender
2. Take about 2 cups of liquid from broth
3. Blend until smooth
4. Pour back into the broth

### Make tacos

1. Take a tortilla and dip it in the broth so its completely covered
2. Place it in a frying pan on medium heat
3. Cover tortilla with shredded cheese and let melt
4. Place shredded meat on one side of the tortilla
5. Drizzle some broth on top of the meat
6. Flip over half the tortilla to make a little meat and cheese envelope
